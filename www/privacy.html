Bastion TD — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

The short version

Bastion TD collects no personal data whatsoever. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no network requests made by the game.

Data storage

Your game progress (stars, research points, hero levels, achievements, settings) is saved only on your own device using local storage. It never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app or use the in-game "Reset all progress" option.

Permissions

The app requests no special permissions: no location, no contacts, no camera, no microphone, no storage access beyond its own sandbox.

Children

Because no data is collected from anyone, no data is collected from children.

Contact

Questions about this policy: open an issue at github.com/OzSpidey/Bastion-TD.
