❓ How to Play

Basics

Enemies march from the spawn to the exit. Each leak costs lives. On path maps, towers are built on the stone sockets lining the road, so choose them wisely (Banks and Beacons take up a socket too). Maze maps let you build on any open tile. Earn cash from kills and survive every wave.

Towers

Every tower has two upgrade paths with three tiers each, and you can invest in both. Click a tower to upgrade, sell, or change its targeting priority (First / Last / Strong / Weak / Air / Close).

Enemy traits

Flying enemies are ignored by Cannons. Stealth enemies can only be targeted near a Beacon or by a Recon Sniper. Armor reduces every hit, so use Venom (ignores armor) or armor-piercing upgrades. Bosses arrive every 10th wave.

Heroes

Pick one of five heroes on the map-select screen. Your hero fights on the battlefield: click them to select (H), then click anywhere to move them. Heroes block enemies on contact and level up from kills made near them. Heroes cast their signature ability manually: click the ability button next to the selected hero (or the Cast button in the side panel). If they fall, they respawn after 12 seconds.

Synergies, boons & combos

Towers on adjacent tiles can link into one of 12 discoverable synergies (a gold ring hints when a placement would link). Every 5th wave offers a boon draft: pick 1 of 3 run-long bonuses. Chained kills build a combo that pays bonus cash, towers earn veteran ranks from their own kills, and any combat tower can be overcharged ($60) to fire twice as fast for 6 seconds. Watch for golden elite enemies (triple bounty) and purple omen waves (+50% HP, double cash).

Iron Challenges

Earn a star on a campaign map to unlock its Iron Challenge: one life, 20 waves, two tower types banned, and no commander abilities. Clear it for a permanent badge and +60 RP.

Curses, relics & hazards

Accept optional curse contracts on map select for +25% Research Points each. Rare enemies carry a glowing relic: kill them before they escape to claim a free boon. Tap the glowing vents on the battlefield ($90) for a massive eruption. Double-maxed towers can Ascend ($800) into a golden super-form, and towers that reach veteran rank ★★★ earn themselves a name. When your lives drop to 3, your army triggers Last Stand: every tower overcharges free, once per game. Endless mode gains a stacking mutator every 10 waves.

Maze mode

The field is open. Your towers physically block the path, so build long corridors to maximize damage. You cannot fully wall off the exit.

Abilities

Four commander abilities on cooldown: Airstrike (click target — the meteor lands 2s later), Frost Nova (global slow), Overclock (all towers fire 2x faster) and Reinforcements (click a spot — 2 militia hold it for 20s).

Hotkeys

1–9 pick tower · Space pause · F game speed · N send next wave · U/I upgrade selected · T targeting · O overcharge selected · S sell · Esc deselect

Research

Every cleared wave earns Research Points. Spend them in the Research Lab on permanent perks: more cash, more lives, stronger towers, interest income and faster abilities.
