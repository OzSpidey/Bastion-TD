❓ How to Play

Basics

Enemies march from the spawn to the exit. Each leak costs lives. Build towers on open tiles, earn cash from kills, and survive every wave.

Towers

Every tower has two upgrade paths with three tiers each, and you can invest in both. Click a tower to upgrade, sell, or change its targeting priority (First / Last / Strong / Close).

Enemy traits

Flying enemies are ignored by Cannons. Stealth enemies can only be targeted near a Beacon or by a Recon Sniper. Armor reduces every hit, so use Venom (ignores armor) or armor-piercing upgrades. Bosses arrive every 10th wave.

Heroes

Pick one of five heroes on the map-select screen. Your hero fights on the battlefield: click them to select (H), then click anywhere to move them. Heroes block enemies on contact, level up from kills made near them, and auto-cast a signature ability. If they fall, they respawn after 12 seconds.

Maze mode

The field is open. Your towers physically block the path, so build long corridors to maximize damage. You cannot fully wall off the exit.

Abilities

Three commander abilities on cooldown: Airstrike (click target), Frost Nova (global slow) and Overclock (all towers fire 2x faster).

Hotkeys

1–9 pick tower · Space pause · F game speed · N send next wave · U/I upgrade selected · T targeting · S sell · Esc deselect

Research

Every cleared wave earns Research Points. Spend them in the Research Lab on permanent perks: more cash, more lives, stronger towers, interest income and faster abilities.
